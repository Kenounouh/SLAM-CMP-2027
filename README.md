# SLAM-CMP-2027: School on Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence for Many-body and Condensed Matter Physics 2027

## Introduction

SLAM-CMP 2027 (School on Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence for Many-body and Condensed Matter Physics 2027) is a seven-day residential school bringing together lattices and neural networks, condensed matter physicists and machine-learning practitioners, Germany and Cameroon.
