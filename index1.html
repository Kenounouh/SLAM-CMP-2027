Welcome

We are pleased to invite you to the School on Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence for Many-body and Condensed Matter Physics 2027 (SLAM-CMP 2027), to be held at the University of Dschang, Cameroon, from March 22–28, 2027.

Funded by: The "Bridging Minds" program of the Max Planck Gesellschaft (Max Planck Society) within their Max Planck Africa initiative, with additional support from the Max Planck Institute for the Science of Light.
